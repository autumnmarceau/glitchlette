PRESS KIT


Since 2017, GLITCHLETTE has been the sonic moniker of Autumn Marceau, a self-taught producer, songwriter, and vocalist based in Seattle, WA. GLITCHLETTE crafts cinematic, beat-driven songs that swing between tender ballads and explosive breakdowns, lush dirges and mosh-pit anthems punctuated by ethereal vocals both soft and belted.
