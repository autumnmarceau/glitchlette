ABOUT GLITCHLETTE



crygaze pop for final girls

Since 2017, GLITCHLETTE has been the sonic moniker of Autumn Marceau, a producer, songwriter, and vocalist based in Seattle, WA. GLITCHLETTE crafts cinematic, beat-driven songs that swing between tender ballads and explosive breakdowns, lush dirges and mosh-pit anthems punctuated by ethereal vocals both soft and belted. After teaching herself electronic production from the ground up, she released her debut EP SUBSOIL in 2018.

In 2020, Marceau collaborated with Default Genders on the remix album Pain Mop Girl, adding vocals and additional production to the song checking in with the old gang.

Her latest work, titled EVERY RAT WANTS TO CRAWL IN YOUR MOUTH AND CLEAN YOUR TEETH!, is set to release in September 2025. Written between September 2019 and May 2020, EVERY RAT is a short collection of diary entries inspired by House of Leaves, two breakups, the film Mandy, and manic episodes.

Built around irregular song structures and progressive arcs, EVERY RAT borrows the language of cinema: tension and release, tonal shifts, and emotional swells more akin to scene changes than traditional pop structure. Though softer in sound than a typical slasher soundtrack, EVERY RAT carries the emotional viscera and ethos of psychological horror: the monster is never seen, only felt.
