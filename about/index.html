ABOUT GLITCHLETTE



skygaze pop for final girls

Since 2017, GLITCHLETTE has been the sonic moniker of Autumn Marceau, a self-taught producer, songwriter, and vocalist based in Seattle, WA. Her sound layers ethereal vocal acrobatics, genre-bending drumwork and body-crushing bass, twisting elements of shoegaze, IDM, art pop, dream pop, darkwave and harsh noise into a sound all her own. A lifelong multi-instrumentalist, GLITCHLETTE taught herself electronic production from the ground up and released her debut EP SUBSOIL in 2018.

In 2020, Marceau collaborated with Default Genders on her remix album Pain Mop Girl, adding vocals and additional production to the song checking in with the old gang.

Her latest work EVERY RAT WANTS TO CRAWL IN YOUR MOUTH AND CLEAN YOUR TEETH! EP, is set to release in September 2025. Written between September 2019 and May 2020, EVERY RAT is a short collection of diary entries inspired by House of Leaves , two breakups, the film Mandy, and three separate manic episodes.

EVERY RAT is built around irregular song structures and progressive arcs. Influenced by film scores, these songs borrow the language of cinema: tension and release, tonal shifts, and emotional swells more akin to scene changes than traditional pop structure. Though softer in sound than your typical slasher soundtrack, EVERY RAT carries the emotional viscera and ethos of psychological horror: the monster is never seen, only felt.
