FREQUENT INQUIRIES

Who Makes The Music?

GLITCHLETTE is the sole producer, composer and performer of the music, unless otherwise noted.

What Software/Plugins/Hardware Does GLITCHLETTE Use?

GLITCHLETTE primarily uses:

  • Digital Audio Workstation (DAW): Ableton Live
  • Most Used Plugins: Serum, SoundToys Little Plate, Massive, BlackRoosterAudio plugin bundle
  • Hardware: Korg Minilogue, Telefunken M80 Mic, Push 2

What is the meaning of EVERY RAT's title?

About a month before I started writing what would be the EP, I had to have emergency dental surgery to remove all of my wisdom teeth. The infection had started to spread to my airways and jawbone, and I was nearly hospitalized.

The days that followed are an opioid-induced fever dream but on the other end of it I had "next ep title: every rat wants to crawl in your mouth and clean your teeth" on my notes app.

I'm still not sure what it means, but it felt right at the time, and by the time I considered changing it, I'd grown too attached.
